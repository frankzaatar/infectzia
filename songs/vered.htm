Vered (Rose)

Vered is climbing on the roofs all day long
She is standing quietly
Looking at cars
And choosing politely
Who is the least worthwhile to land on today

 

And all the friends
Who are standing now at the intersection
Are waiting for her to jump downwards without a fright
'cause you can always nit the body
to the face with a sewing thread

Vered is paralyzed from shoulder to toes
She is limping quietly, to a multy-story building
Didn't suffice to get well and already tries to bring forward death

 

(Bozo-מעברית)
